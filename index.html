YOGA

Yoga is essentially a spiritual discipline based on an extremely subtle science,
which focuses on bringing harmony between mind and body. It is an art and scince of healthy living.
The word 'Yoga' is derived from the Sanskrit root 'Yuj', meaning 'to join' or 'to yoke' or 'to unite'.




ASANAS

An asana is a body posture, originally and still a general term for a sitting meditation pose, and later extended in hatha yoga and modern yoga as exercise, to any type of pose or position, adding reclining, standing, inverted, twisting, and balancing poses.
